# Business Impact Summary

This document translates the Global Store Retail Performance & Profitability Analysis into practical business decisions. The goal is to show how the analysis can support management actions, not just describe historical data.

## Executive Business Message

Global Store generated strong revenue, but the analysis shows that sales growth is not always profitable growth. The main business risks are margin leakage from loss-making products, aggressive discounting, and regional performance gaps.

## Priority Findings and Actions

| Priority | Finding | Business Risk | Recommended Action | Expected Business Impact |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Tables generated strong sales but negative profit | Revenue is masking a loss-making product group | Review Tables pricing, supplier cost, shipping cost, and discount policy | Reduce recurring margin leakage |
| 2 | Discounts above 30% are linked with negative margin | Promotional strategy may be destroying profit | Add approval rules for deep discounts and monitor discount bands in Power BI | Protect gross margin and reduce unprofitable sales |
| 3 | Southeast Asia has high sales but weak profit | Regional growth may not be commercially healthy | Review product mix, shipping cost, and discount levels in Southeast Asia | Improve regional profit quality |
| 4 | Technology is the strongest category by sales and profit | Profitable growth opportunity may be under-prioritized | Prioritize high-margin Technology products in campaigns and inventory planning | Grow revenue without weakening margin |
| 5 | Consumer drives scale while Home Office has stronger margin | Customer segments require different strategies | Use Consumer for scale campaigns and Home Office for margin-focused targeting | Improve customer value and campaign efficiency |

## Business Value of the Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard helps management monitor:

- total sales and total profit
- profit margin quality
- category and sub-category performance
- loss-making products
- discount risk
- customer segment performance
- regional sales and profitability
- shipping cost and order priority trends
