# Executive Summary

Global Store generated **12.64M in sales** and **1.47M in profit** across **51,290 transaction rows**. Overall margin is **11.61%**.

The analysis shows that the business has strong sales volume, but margin quality is uneven. Technology is the strongest category, while Furniture needs closer margin control. The Tables sub-category is the clearest issue, producing about **757K in sales** but **-64K in profit**.

Discounting is the biggest profit risk. Orders without discounts produce about **25.32%** margin, while orders discounted above 30% produce about **-51.27%** margin. Southeast Asia is another concern, with about **884K in sales** and only **17.9K in profit**.

## Business Actions

- Review Tables pricing, discounting, supplier cost, and shipping cost.
- Add tighter controls for discounts above 30%.
- Investigate Southeast Asia product mix, discounts, and shipping cost.
- Expand Technology products through targeted sales campaigns.
- Track sales and margin together in Power BI.
